How it works

1. Upload

Upload a PGN or paste a FEN. You can also add a link from your favorite platform.

2. Choose depth

Pick engine and depth. Stockfish for raw strength. Lc0 for neural style. Set time per move if you prefer.

3. Run on AI compute

Your job is routed to decentralized GPU nodes on HyperCycle. No hardware to manage.

4. Get results

Download an annotated PGN and a visual PDF with eval graphs and key moments.
